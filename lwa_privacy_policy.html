Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

Overview

This privacy policy explains how Voartex Amazon Ads Manager ("we," "our," or "the Application") handles data when you authorize our application to access your Amazon Advertising account through the Amazon Advertising API.

Information We Access

When you authorize our application, we access the following information from your Amazon Advertising account:

  • Campaign data (names, budgets, targeting settings, status)
  • Performance metrics (impressions, clicks, spend, sales, ACOS)
  • Keyword and product targeting information
  • Ad group and product ad details

How We Use Your Information

We use the information accessed from your Amazon Advertising account solely to:

  • Provide campaign management and optimization services
  • Generate performance reports and analytics
  • Create and modify advertising campaigns as authorized by you
  • Monitor and optimize advertising performance

Data Storage and Security

Your Amazon Advertising data is:

  • Accessed only when needed to perform authorized services
  • Not sold, shared, or distributed to third parties
  • Protected using industry-standard security practices
  • Retained only as long as necessary to provide services or as required by law

Your Rights

You maintain full control over your data:

  • You can revoke application access at any time through your Amazon Advertising account settings
  • You can request deletion of any stored data by contacting us
  • You can request information about what data we have accessed

Third-Party Services

This application uses Amazon's Advertising API to access your advertising data. Your use of Amazon's services is governed by Amazon's own privacy policies and terms of service.
